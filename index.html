Games

The TOAST LADS are a school based development team working on games for the Oregon Game Project Challenge. They have competed in season 16, where they won the Best In Show award for the High School Division. They are currently competing in season 17.

Season 16: Theme: 'Breaking the Conventions' Game: SE/\/SE
Se/\/Se (Sense) is a strategy, maze, horror game where you use your five senses to escape a mansion full of monsters. The senses you cherish most are ripped from you one by one and you must now survive without it. Make it through all five levels to escape. Will you navigate your way through the danger?
The game can purchased to play for yourself on Steam
Listen to the Official Soundtrack! on Youtube!

Season 17 - Theme: 'Cycles' Game: M/\RK3T M3T/\L
M/\RK3T M3T/\L (Market Metal) is a 2D Survival and Strategy game, taking place on a train that is your lifeline. You must scavenge for resources to survive and upgrade your trains ability to produce for you, and defend you. As you travel along the tracks, you may encounter outposts, where you can trade for resources. You will also encounter train stations, where enemy robots are lurking, waiting to attack you, and you have to defend against them.
